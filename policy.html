PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy describes how NTA Models SA Scouting Division ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our scouting services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Details: Your full name, date of birth, gender, height, and weight.
  • Contact Information: Your email address and phone number.
  • Social Media Handles: Your Instagram and TikTok usernames.
  • Application Information: Details about the category you are applying for and any courses or services you are interested in.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To review and evaluate your application for our scouting services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your application status and potential opportunities.
  • To provide you with information about our services, events, and news.
  • To improve our scouting process and user experience.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • NTA Models and Talent Academy: For the purpose of managing your application and providing you with relevant academy services.
  • Partner Agencies: If we believe your profile is a good fit for a reputable partner agency, we may share your information to help secure a placement for you. We will always seek your consent before sharing your information with any third party.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in our contact section.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7. Contact Us
