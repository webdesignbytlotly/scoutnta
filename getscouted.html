IMPORTANT NOTICE

The scouting service provided by NTA Models SA is free for all applicants initially. Our goal is to help you get signed with a professional modeling agency.

We charge a R19.00 (ZAR) administrative fee to process applications from models who are not scouted directly by our agents and are representing themselves independently. This is a once-off, non-refundable charge that helps us process applications efficiently and invest time in models who are serious.

Important Payment Note: This admin fee is only paid on our official website domain: scout.ntamodels.co.za. We will never provide you with banking details to deposit money, and we do not provide links to external websites for payment.

If you are successfully signed with an agency through our scouting efforts, you agree to pay NTA Models SA a 10% commission fee from the first two successful bookings you receive with that agency. This fee is paid directly to us and is separate from any commissions or fees the agency may charge you. This allows us to continue our scouting efforts and support new talent.

To report any fraud or confirm agent identity please email: nta.models@ntamodels.co.za

Required Images and Submission Protocol

To ensure we can assess your potential accurately, please adhere strictly to the following requirements:

  • Submit four clear, unedited, and recent photos.
  • Photos should be taken in natural light against a plain, uncluttered background.
  • Your face must be clearly visible, with no heavy makeup, filters, sunglasses, or hats.
  • We do not accept professional photos at this stage; we need to see your natural look.
  • All required images and accompanying intro videos must be uploaded to a public Google Drive folder or hosted using a publicly accessible link (self-hosted or otherwise). We must be able to view them without signing in.
  • The administrative fee of R19.00 (ZAR) applies to applications submitted without an NTA Agent Code.
  • All applicants under the age of 18 must secure explicit written consent from a parent or legal guardian before submitting their application.
