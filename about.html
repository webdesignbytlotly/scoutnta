ABOUT

Established in 2025, the NTA Models SA Scouting Division is the dedicated talent discovery arm of NTA Models and Talent Academy, a leading name in South Africa's fashion and entertainment industry. Our primary focus is to find and develop the next generation of top-tier models and talent.

Our aim is to identify individuals with unique potential, raw charisma, and the ambition to succeed. We believe that true talent can be found anywhere, and we are committed to providing a platform for aspiring models from all backgrounds to launch their careers.

Our vision is to become the premier scouting agency in Africa, known for our ethical practices, personalized approach, and ability to cultivate enduring careers for the models we discover. We work tirelessly to scout and groom talent, ensuring they are prepared for the demands of the global market. Our network and expertise serve as a bridge, connecting fresh faces with prestigious international and local agencies, ensuring a successful and sustainable journey in the industry.

We support FAIRe Movement

FAIRe is a foundation and movement created to protect the rights, identities, and livelihoods of real human models in an industry increasingly threatened by artificial intelligence.
